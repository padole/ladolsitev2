Port Services & Cargo Handling

LADOL is registered as an ISPS-certified port facility with IMO and receives local and international vessels. Featuring a 200m quay, 8.5m draft, 25-ton/m2 high load bearing area and additional 30-ton bollards at either end, LADOL’s quay can accommodate up to six supply vessels and three Heavy Lift Vessels simultaneously with 24 hours a day operation.

Welding, Fabrication and New Build Services

Established in 2020 to develop capacity for marine and structural steel fabrication, marine vessel repairs, and construction for Inland waterways and coastal vessels. With fully equipped, certified welding crews our expertise covers steel and aluminum works, piping, machining, structural fabrication up to 200 tons, offshore equipment installation, and more.

Why Choose Ladol

Ideal location in Lagos harbour

The location of the Base and the services it offers will enable operator and contractors to maximize their operational efficiency. Manufacturers setting up on the base will find LADOL has excellent infrastructure

Health, Safety and Environment

LADOL operates an Integrated Management System (IMS) that includes a quality, health, safety and environmental management system. Our HSE Policy focuses on delivering a superior service to our customers that exceeds their expectations.

General infrastructure & Security

The Base uses the latest infrastructure, IT and personnel security solutions. All the operational and personnel areas are fenced and can only be accessed through secure access control offices.

Secure, Modern Closed and Open Storage Areas

All our storage areas are built to international standards and suitable for both storage and small fabrication works. These areas can also be extensively customized for project work and specialized storage, such as overhead cranes, racking, bund walls and epoxy floors, etc.

Office Complex

LADOL has a range of facilities including office buildings and complexes in a secured area, operational offices built above warehouses, workshops next to the quay, and new Portacabins. All offices come fully furnished and serviced, including high-speed Internet and catering provision.

Personnel Transportation

LADOL operates scheduled passenger transport for staff and workers. This ferry service runs on a set timetable, several times a day, from secure locations on the mainland. The modern and comfortable passenger boats are exclusively for LADOL staff and workers.

ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
