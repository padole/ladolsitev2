Independent Power Generation

Overview

Reliable, Sustainable Energy for Industrial Growth

As an IPP company, we operate a dedicated 6.3MW power plant that delivers both primary and backup electricity. Our energy solutions guarantee uninterrupted operations, eliminating downtime and creating an enabling ecosystem for industrial growth.

Our power infrastructure is engineered for uptime and resilience — built with redundant backup systems, continuous system monitoring, and real-time performance optimisation. This ensures that clients’ critical operations remain powered, efficient, and secure, no matter the demand or conditions.

Driving Sustainable Industrialisation Beyond the Zone

Beyond serving internal operations, LADOL’s energy system is strategically positioned to power future growth within and around the Free Zone. With expansion plans to supply commercial power to neighbouring industries, the facility represents a key step in developing a sustainable energy hub for Nigeria’s industrial sector.

This forward-looking approach reduces dependence on the national grid while promoting cleaner, more efficient power generation that aligns with LADOL’s broader vision for green industrialisation and economic development.

Features

6.3MW installed capacity serving both primary and backup needs

24/7 continuous, uninterrupted power supply

Fully compliant with international safety and efficiency standards

Continuous system monitoring and performance optimisation

Redundant backup systems ensuring zero downtime

Sustainable power model supporting Free Zone and external industries

ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
