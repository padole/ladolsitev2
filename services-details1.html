Port Services & Cargo Handling

Overview

Efficient, Secure & Cost-Effective Logistics for Energy and Industrial Projects

LADOL delivers world-class port and cargo handling services tailored to meet the demanding logistics needs of oil producers, service providers, and industrial operators. As an ISPS-certified port facility registered with the International Maritime Organisation (IMO), LADOL is equipped to receive both local and international vessels, ensuring smooth, compliant, and secure port operations.

Our integrated logistics solutions cover every stage of cargo management — from vessel arrival and offloading to stacking, storage, and final delivery. Through localised services and efficient coordination, we help clients reduce transportation costs, minimise cargo risks, and improve turnaround times for both offshore and inland projects.

Comprehensive Port & Cargo Handling Solutions

Receiving & Dispatching

End-to-end management of incoming and outgoing cargo with full documentation, inspection, and supervision to ensure timely, error-free operations.

Stacking & Racking

Organised and secure cargo storage in dedicated stacking areas designed to optimise space utilisation and facilitate easy retrieval.

Loading & Unloading

Supported by heavy-lift cranes, forklifts, and modern handling equipment, we guarantee the safe and efficient transfer of goods between vessels, trucks, and barges.

Localised Services & Supplies

On-demand access to essential materials, logistics support, and local supply chains reduces dependency on distant sources and cuts logistics costs.

Supervised Delivery

Every cargo movement is closely monitored by our experienced supervisors to ensure accurate, secure, and timely delivery to its final destination.

Benefits

Cost Efficiency – Reduced transportation and handling costs through localised solutions

Safety & Reliability – Minimised risk of cargo damage with strict handling standards

Seamless Operations – End-to-end visibility from port entry to final delivery

Industry Expertise – Dedicated support for oil, gas, marine, and infrastructure projects

Facilities & Capabilities

  • Dedicated stacking and racking areas for bulk and project cargo
  • Heavy-lift cranes, forklifts, and transport vehicles for safe movement
  • Quayside access for direct vessel loading/unloading
  • Hardstanding areas for assembly and staging
  • Experienced logistics supervisors and trained handlers
  • Integrated support for offshore, inland, and onshore logistics
ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
