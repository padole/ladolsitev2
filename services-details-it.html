IT Infrastructure & Managed Services

Overview

Secure, Scalable, Always Available

We provide robust, end-to-end IT solutions that empower business continuity, connectivity and innovation — both onshore and offshore. Our infrastructure and managed services are designed to ensure your operations stay secure, efficient and uninterrupted at every level.

With advanced data centre facilities, 24/7 monitoring, and expert technical support, we serve as the technology backbone for energy, marine, and industrial clients managing complex projects across Nigeria and beyond.

Built for Business Continuity

Our IT infrastructure and management services enable organisations to focus on their core objectives while we handle the complexities of technology management.

From hardware to cloud, and from networks to cybersecurity, we deliver scalable, future-ready solutions that support critical operations around the clock.

We don’t just manage technology — we make it work smarter for your business.

Key Services

Data centre Colocation

Host mission-critical systems in secure, climate-controlled facilities with redundant power and connectivity. Our data centre are engineered for uptime and operational resilience.

Outsourced IT Support & Help Ddesk

Our professional team provides dedicated remote and on-site support to keep your systems running smoothly. From troubleshooting to user support, we ensure your teams remain productive.

24/7 Network Monitoring & Connectivity

With 24-hour power supply connected to our solar-integrated grid, LADOL ensures uninterrupted energy for all operations. Continuous system monitoring and performance optimisation keep your facilities connected, efficient and secure — minimising downtime and eliminating service disruptions.

Cybersecurity & Compliance

End-to-end protection through robust security architecture, proactive monitoring and compliance with industry standards ensures your data and assets remain safe at all times.

ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
