Hazardous & Non-Hazardous Waste Management

Overview

Safe, Compliant & Sustainable.

We provide specialised waste management solutions for both hazardous and non-hazardous waste streams generated during drilling, workover, and other industrial operations. Our approach combines environmental responsibility with regulatory compliance, ensuring all waste materials are handled, treated, and disposed of in line with global best practices and Nigerian environmental standards. At LADOL, sustainability is integrated into every stage of our operations. We manage waste streams efficiently to reduce environmental impact, safeguard personnel and facilities, and support our clients’ commitment to safe, responsible operations.

Comprehensive Waste Management Solutions

Our integrated waste management system is designed to serve the needs of oil and gas operators, service companies, and industrial clients. We handle every phase from waste collection and containment to treatment, recovery, and environmentally safe disposal.

Our Capabilities Include
  • Hazardous Waste Management - collection, segregation, treatment, and safe disposal of drilling muds, chemical residues, oily wastes, contaminated soils, and industrial by-products.
  • Non-Hazardous Waste Handling - management of general, domestic, and recyclable waste generated from operational and residential facilities.
  • Waste Segregation & Containment - properly labelled containment systems for different waste types to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Waste Treatment & Recovery - thermal, biological, and mechanical treatment options that maximise resource recovery and minimise landfill use.
  • Regulatory Compliance & Reporting - full documentation and traceability in line with DPR, NESREA, and ISO environmental standards.

Sustainability in Action

Our goal is not just compliance — but leadership in sustainable operations.

We continually improve waste reduction and recycling initiatives, integrating environmentally responsible practices that align with our clients’ ESG goals.

By managing both hazardous and non-hazardous waste effectively, we help create cleaner, safer work environments while protecting surrounding ecosystems.

ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
