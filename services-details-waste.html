Hazardous & Non-Hazardous Waste Management

Overview

Safe, Compliant & Sustainable.

At LADOL, we provide specialised waste management solutions for both hazardous and non-hazardous waste streams generated during drilling, workover, and other industrial operations. Our approach combines environmental responsibility with strict regulatory compliance, ensuring all waste materials are handled, treated, and disposed of in line with global best practices and Nigerian environmental standards

Comprehensive Waste Management Capabilities

We combine advanced treatment technology with robust compliance frameworks, enabling traceability, documentation, and reporting aligned with DPR, NESREA, and ISO environmental standards. This ensures every waste management activity supports both client objectives and national sustainability goals.

Key Features
  • Collection, segregation, treatment, and safe disposal of drilling muds, chemical residues, oily wastes, contaminated soils, and other industrial by-products.
  • Efficient management of general, domestic, and recyclable waste from operational and residential areas.
  • Thermal, biological, and mechanical processes designed to maximise recycling and minimise landfill dependency.
  • Continuous improvement of waste reduction initiatives supporting client ESG goals and cleaner operational environments.
ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
