Material Handling

Overview

Efficient Handling. Precision. Heavy-Lift Expertise.

At LADOL, we deliver specialised material handling solutions designed to support the most demanding offshore, industrial and project-cargo operations. Located at the heart of Nigeria’s logistics hub, our facilities, equipment and experienced team are fully prepared to manage bulk cargo, oversized units and heavy-lift loads with speed, safety and reliability.

Our Material Handling Capabilities

  • Heavy-Lift & Project Cargo Handling - Equipped for modules, reels, engines and structural units with lifting capacities reaching 600 tonnes in partnership with heavy-lift experts.
  • Special Materials Handling Facilities - Includes high load-bearing quays, large laydown and rigging areas designed for heavy and oversized cargoes.
  • Storage, Stacking & Racking Solutions - Secure and adaptable storage facilities for bulk, break-bulk, project and general cargo, enabling efficient inventory, access and retrieval.
  • Integrated Logistics & On-Site Equipment - Comprehensive fleet including cranes, forklifts, trucks and trailers to handle material movement from receipt through to delivery.
  • Vessel-to-Shore and Onward Transport Support - From quay unloading, staging and transhipment to barge, truck or vessel onward delivery, we manage the full movement chain.

Benefits

Project-Ready Infrastructure – Built to meet heavy-cargo and offshore logistics demands.

End-to-End Service –Handling from arrival, storage, stacking, to final delivery.

Reduced Handling Risk & Cost –Robust equipment and layouts minimise cargo damage and maximise efficiency.

Operational Speed – Dedicated support for oil, gas, marine, and infrastructure projects

Facility Highlights

  • High load capacity quay and yard areas engineered for heavy cargo handling.
  • Certified heavy-lift capacity and strategic partnerships expanding lifting capability
  • Round-the-clock material handling operations to meet project timeframes.
  • Highly trained rigging and cargo handling personnel experienced in offshore and industrial logistics.
ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbor, LADOL provides a one-stop-shop for multinational industrial and oil and gas companies operating in West Africa.
