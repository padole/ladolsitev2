Welcome to LADOL

LADOL operates West Africa’s only integrated logistics base and Sustainable Industrial Special Economic Zone (SSEZ). Having transformed a swamp into a world-class industrial space, it is now transitioning into the world’s first sustainable industrial SEZ (SSEZ), operating a structured industrial ecosystem and circular economy, which will not only ensure commercial competitiveness for its tenants, but will also make LADOL Free Zone the blueprint for sustainable industrialisation in Africa, aiming to achieve net zero by 2035.

This state-of-the-art free zone is strategically located at the point of entry into Lagos Harbour, and provides fully serviced facilities and heavy infrastructure capable of supporting the largest industrial projects in the world.

LADOL is committed to the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals and has been developed for the long term to support sustainable industrialisation across Nigeria, driving both local and global prosperity.

Our Mission
  • Build a Sustainable Industrial Special Economic Zone with robust and technologically advanced infrastructure that can operate safely and efficiently 24/7 to support high-value industrial projects.
  • Reduce the costs of operating in Nigeria by 50%.
  • Be a transparent and ethical provider of services to local and international companies.
  • Attract a diverse range of companies to set up, engineer, manufacture and operate in the zone, servicing both the local and international markets.
Our Vision
  • A sustainable ecosystem filled with a diverse range of international and local industrial companies, engineering and manufacturing in Nigeria for the first time, servicing the Nigerian and African markets.
  • The success of LADOL attracts USD 100 billion of new investment into Nigeria.
  • Hundreds of thousands of direct and indirect jobs are created as Nigeria becomes the industrial hub for West Africa, leading to Nigeria joining the G20.

LADOL’s Management System and Standards

LADOL was the first company in West and North Africa to be certified to ISO 45001:2018, ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 Management Systems. It is also one of the safest terminals with ISPS Certification.

At our core, we believe safety is a shared responsibility; everyone plays a part. By empowering our workforce and fostering open consultation with line managers, we’ve built a strong culture of safety that drives performance across the board. Our proven track record in preventing workplace injuries and protecting assets continues to earn the trust and recognition of our clients.

ISO 9001:2015

We operate under a fully certified ISO 9001:2015 Management Quality System. This ensures consistent service delivery, strong process control, and continuous improvement across all operations.

ISO 45001:2018

We are certified to the ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health standards. This certification shows our commitment to safe operations, risk reduction, and a proactive safety culture. We protect our people, partners, and environment every day.

ISO:14001:2015

We are certified to the ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management standards. This demonstrates our commitment to responsible operations and pollution control. We actively manage environmental risks and improve sustainability performance.

ISPS Level 1

We operate in full compliance with ISPS Code Security Level 1. This ensures a secure, controlled and safe environment for all port users. Access, monitoring and protective measures are actively maintained. Your people, assets and cargo remain protected at all times.
