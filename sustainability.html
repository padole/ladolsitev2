Building a Sustainable Future

Environmental, Social & Governance leadership aligned with global SDGs

We are committed to responsible operations that protect the environment, support people and create long-term value for all stakeholders. Our sustainability practices are guided by global ESG principles and aligned with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our Sustainability Approach

Sustainability is at the core of how we operate. We prioritise efficient resource use, safe operations and responsible growth that respects both the environment and our host community. Through continuous improvement, transparent reporting and strong governance systems, we aim to drive positive change across all areas of our business.

Our approach focuses on developing resilient operations that reduce carbon impact, enhance workplace safety, and foster social and economic development. By integrating sustainability into every decision, we ensure our facilities, projects and partnerships contribute to long-term progress.

ESG Framework

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint through responsible resource management, pollution control and sustainable operational practices. Our initiatives include:

  • Waste segregation and environmentally safe disposal
  • Energy-efficient operations
  • Emission reduction strategies
  • Water conservation and responsible chemical handling

Social Responsibility

People and communities are central to our mission. We invest in programmes that promote safety, well-being, and shared prosperity. Our social commitments include:

  • Safe and healthy working environments
  • Local employment and skills development
  • Community support and outreach programmes
  • Equal opportunity and employee welfare initiatives

Governance & Ethics

Strong governance ensures transparency, accountability and long-term operational excellence. We maintain:

  • Compliance with ISO Standards (9001, 14001, 45001)
  • Ethical business conduct and anti-corruption policies
  • Clear reporting structures and stakeholder engagement
  • Commitment to international best practices and regulatory requirements

Alignment with the United Nations' SDGs

Our sustainability strategy is aligned with key UN SDGs, focusing on areas where we can make meaningful contributions

Social & Community Commitment

Our responsibility extends beyond our facility. We actively support local communities through initiatives that enhance quality of life, economic empowerment and education.

Our social programs include:

  • Community health and safety awareness campaigns
  • Support for local schools and educational institutions
  • Female empowerment and leadership development initiatives
  • Support for local businesses and suppliers
