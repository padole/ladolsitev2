Material Handling

Overview

Efficient Handling, Precision & Heavy-Lift Expertise.

At LADOL, we provide end-to-end material handling solutions built to meet the demands of offshore, industrial and heavy-project logistics. Strategically located at Nigeria’s deep offshore logistics hub, our facilities are equipped to manage everything from bulk and break-bulk cargo to oversized units and heavy-lift components.

With purpose-built quays, reinforced yards and round-the-clock operations, LADOL ensures the safe and efficient movement of materials from arrival through to final delivery. Every lift, transfer and storage operation is executed with precision, safety and speed, reducing project delays and maintaining asset integrity.

Facility Highlights

  • High-load capacity quay and yard areas engineered for heavy cargo handling.
  • Certified heavy-lift capacity and strategic partnerships expanding lifting capability.
  • Round-the-clock material handling operations to meet project timeframes.
  • Highly trained rigging and cargo handling personnel experienced in offshore and industrial logistics.

Our Material Handling Capabilities

Vessel-to-Shore & Onward Transport Support

From quay unloading, staging and transhipment to barge, truck or vessel onward delivery, we manage the full movement chain.

Special Materials-Handling Facilities

Includes high load-bearing quays, large laydown and rigging areas designed for heavy and oversized cargoes.

Storage, Stacking & Racking Solutions

Secure and adaptable storage facilities for bulk, break-bulk, project and general cargo, enabling efficient inventory, access and retrieval.

Integrated Logistics & On-Site Equipment

Comprehensive fleet including cranes, forklifts, trucks and trailers to handle material movement from receipt through to delivery.

Benefits

Project-Ready Infrastructure – Built to meet heavy-cargo and offshore logistics demands.

End-to-End Service –Handling from arrival, storage, stacking, to final delivery.

Reduced Handling Risk & Cost –Robust equipment and layouts minimise cargo damage and maximise efficiency.

Operational Speed – Dedicated support for oil, gas, marine, and infrastructure projects

ABOUT

Positioned strategically at the entry point to Lagos harbour, LADOL operates Africa’s Leading Logistics Hub and Sustainable Industrial Special Economic Zone, providing a one-stop-shop for multinational companies.
