Independent Power Generation

Overview

Reliable Energy for Seamless Operations

As an IPP company, we operate a dedicated 6.3MW power plant that delivers both primary and backup electricity. Our energy solutions guarantee uninterrupted operations, eliminating downtime and creating an enabling ecosystem for industrial growth.

Key Services

  • 6.3MW Capacity - Serving both primary and backup needs.
  • 24/7 Availability - Continuous, uninterrupted power supply.
  • Reliability - Eliminates downtime and boosts efficiency.
  • Sustainability Focus - Supporting growth with clean and efficient energy practices.
