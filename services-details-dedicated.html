Dedicated Equipment & Port Facilities

Overview

Supporting Offshore & Industrial Operations with World-Class Infrastructure

From heavy-lift cranes to purpose-built warehousing and quayside logistics, LADOL provides the infrastructure and equipment backbone that keeps offshore, marine, and industrial projects running smoothly.

Our facilities are engineered for safety, precision, and efficiency — enabling clients to handle even the most demanding operations confidently.

Engineered for Efficiency. Equipped for Every Operation.

At LADOL, we understand that reliable equipment and purpose-built port facilities are the foundation of safe and efficient operations.

Our comprehensive suite of dedicated equipment, warehousing, and heavy-duty infrastructure supports a wide range of industrial, marine, and offshore activities—helping clients execute projects seamlessly from shore to sea..

We combine state-of-the-art assets with a highly skilled workforce to ensure every operation meets international standards of safety, speed, and precision.

Comprehensive Equipment Capabilities

LADOL offers a full range of material handling and support equipment, maintained and operated under strict quality and safety regimes. Whether it’s vessel loading, offshore supply support, or large-scale project logistics, our facilities are built for performance.

Our Equipment and Facilities Include:
  • Pipe racks and stacking areas for secure and organised material storage
  • Dedicated warehousing and enclosed storage zones
  • Heavy-lift cranes, forklifts, and rigging systems for up to 500 tons
  • Fuel storage, compressors, and generator systems for uninterrupted operations
  • Trucks, trailers, and transporters for safe material movement

Seamless Integration with Port & Marine Services

Our equipment and port facilities work hand-in-hand with LADOL’s broader logistics and marine service ecosystem.

This integration ensures faster cargo handling, reduced transportation costs, and safer project execution—enabling clients to focus on core operations while we handle the complexity.

Safety, Reliability & Compliance

Every LADOL asset is maintained to international standards, including ISO, ISPS, and HSE requirements.

Routine inspections, preventive maintenance, and continuous staff training guarantee safe, reliable, and efficient operations around the clock.

Port Infrastructure Designed for Heavy Operations

LADOL’s ISPS-certified port is designed to handle complex offshore and industrial logistics with precision and safety.

Port Highlights:
  • 200m quay with 8.5m draft, accommodating multiple supply and heavy-lift vessels
  • 25-ton/m² high load-bearing capacity
  • 30-ton bollards at either end for secure mooring
  • Integrated product ducts in the quay structure for rapid fueling and transfer
  • 24/7 berthing operations with experienced crews ensuring quick turnaround

Benefits

Faster turnaround through efficient port operations

Reduced logistics and handling costs

Safer operations through strict compliance and best practices

Reliable, flexible solutions tailored to client needs
