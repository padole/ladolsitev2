Welding, Fabrication and New Build Services

Overview

Building Capacity, Driving Innovation

Established in 2020, FBY has built strong expertise in marine and structural steel fabrication, vessel repairs, and inland/coastal vessel construction. With certified welding crews, modern facilities, and international partnerships, we deliver high-quality, safe, and reliable results.

Future-focused, we are also pioneering sustainable technologies, including the development of 70/100-passenger hybrid solar-powered boats in collaboration with international design associates.

FBY Services

New Builds

Barge and pusher tug construction, vessel docking/undocking up to 120m, and hauling/launching using the air balloon method.

Fabrication & Repairs

Steel structures (up to 200T), hull and deck modifications, offshore equipment installations (ROV), barge repairs, and sea fastening.

Surface Treatment

Grit blasting, sandblasting, mechanical preparation, and protective coatings with airless spray technology.

Fully Specialised Services

Heavy lifts up to 500T, NDT services, tank cleaning, scaffolding and rope access, plus provision of certified welders, engineers, and supervisors.

Facilities

Our fully equipped facilities enable us to handle complex marine and structural projects with efficiency and precision:

Quaysides – 100m (8m draft) and 400m (13m draft) with 70T tower crane

Workshops – Covered fabrication, blasting, and painting shops

Heavy Lifting – Rigging capacity up to 500T plus forklifts, trucks, and transporters

Aluminium Repairs
